About ImageGen

ImageGen is an innovative web application that utilizes AI to generate stunning images based on user prompts. Our goal is to empower creativity and inspire users to explore their imagination.

How It Works

Simply enter a creative prompt into the input field on the homepage, click "Generate Image," and watch as our AI transforms your words into a visual masterpiece!

Features

  • User-friendly interface
  • Real-time image generation
  • Image gallery to showcase your creations
  • Responsive design for all devices
